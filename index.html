Who is Frumo?

Long ago, in a hidden valley where the rivers sang and the meadows shimmered, two legends crossed paths — the noble Doge, with his loyal heart and boundless energy, and the wise Pepe, guardian of laughter and peace. From their unlikely bond came something the world had never seen before — a small, green frog-dog with bright, curious eyes, a floppy tongue, and a golden crown that was just a little too big for his head. They called him Frumo.

Frumo grew up chasing fireflies by the pond and playfully barking at the stars. He was a creature of joy — silly but brave, carefree yet loyal. Over time, whispers of the frog with the golden crown spread beyond the valley. They spoke of his gift: wherever Frumo went, he brought unity, laughter, and a spark of chaos that could light up even the darkest nights. Now, Frumo has leapt beyond that quiet valley, hopping into the hearts of people everywhere. He isn’t here for power or gold — only for fun, friendship, and the wild ride to the moon.
